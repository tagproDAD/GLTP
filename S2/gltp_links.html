Rules Summary

  • Maps are released every 8 days and have assigned point values (1–3 points based on difficulty).
  • Extra points are awarded for fastest time on each map
  • Maps can be completed and submitted for speedruns any time during the season.

VOD Requirements

  • VOD required for all point awards (completion, fastest time).
  • Fastest time VODs must be submitted within 6 hours of completion.
  • No VOD = No points. White names may require verification.

Partial Completions

  • Teams can submit a VOD for partial completion if a match ends early.
  • Commissioners will issue a new map ID with a checkpoint unlocked.
  • No fastest time points awarded for these runs.

Game Settings

  • Gravity: Enabled
  • Respawn: 1 Second (or Instant)
  • Other settings: Follow TagPro defaults unless specified

Keepaway (OFM) Game Rules

  • Teams schedule their own match times — any team can challenge any other team at any time.
  • It's recommended (but not required) to play  3 matches total during the season.
  • A match consists of a  best 2 out of 3 series.
  • Each game is  5 minutes, with the winner being the team with the longest total hold time.
  • Games can be played as  3v3 or 4v4 based on team preference and availability.
  • Alternate gravity maps (e.g. Basketball, Pinecone, NF, Rektangle) may be used if both captains agree before the match.

Fair Play Rules

  • No botting, automation, or macros allowed.
  • All gameplay must be performed live by human players.
